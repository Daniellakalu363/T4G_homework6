      World Food Programme

      The World Food Programme (WFP) is the largest humanitarian organization within the United Nations, providing food assistance to over 152 million people in 120 countries and territories. Our mission is to save lives during emergencies and use foods aid to create a path towards peace, stability and prosperity for those recovering from conflict, disasters and the impact of climate change.
